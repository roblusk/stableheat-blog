## Frontmatter cheat sheet

Required fields and current usage distribution:

- `thumbVariant`: integer 1–10. Most-used today: 7 (×9), 4 (×8), 5 (×7),
  2 (×7), 8 (×6). Variant 10 only used once. Pick to spread color across
  the cards grid.
- `thumbIcon`: Font Awesome name without `fa-` prefix. Existing usage:
  `icicles` (5), `solar-panel`/`snowflake`/`house`/`caravan`/`anchor` (3),
  `water`/`shield-halved`/`sailboat`/`oil-well`/`mountain-sun`/`leaf`/
  `industry`/`building` (2), plus single-uses `wheelchair`, `user`, `tree`,
  `temperature-half`, `table-cells-large`, `spa`.
- `applications`: subset of slugs from `src/data/applications.ts` —
  `home`, `floor`, `commercial`, `rv`, `marine`, `snowmelt`, `roof-de-ice`,
  `solar`, `oil-gas`. A post can list multiple.
- `category`: free-text editorial label (badge on hero). Existing examples:
  "Commercial", "Residential", "Marine", "Snowmelt", "Roof De-Ice",
  "RV/Mobile", "Specialty".
